8 Plausible scenario settings

In this section we present specific scenarios from our simulation settings in which evolution of benefit followed similar patterns to [7]. In this case patients were stratified into risk quarters based on their true baseline risk. Within each risk quarter we constructed boxplots of true benefit.

Figure 8.1: Simulation scenarios that closely follow trials. In this case, we see increasing absolute benefits with increasing baseline risk.

Figure 8.2: Simulation scenarios that closely follow trials. In this case, we see increasing absolute benefits with increasing baseline risk up to the third risk quarter. In the fourth risk quarter this trend is interrupted and benefits are diminished.
